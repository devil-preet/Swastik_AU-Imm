Business Innovation and Investment (provisional) visa (subclass 188)

Unlocking Opportunities: Considering a business venture or investment in Australia?

The Subclass 188 visa streamlines the path for skilled entrepreneurs and investors to establish themselves in Australia. This provisional visa serves as a stepping stone to permanent residency, allowing you to contribute to Australia's dynamic economy while enjoying its exceptional lifestyle.

Multiple Streams Cater to Your Goals:

♦ Business Innovation Stream: Ideal for established business owners with a successful track record.
♦ Investor Stream: Designed for investors seeking to make a significant financial contribution to a designated Australian state or territory.
♦ Significant Investor Stream: Aimed at high-net-worth individuals with substantial investments in complying assets.

Extension options are available for existing visa holders in specific streams.

Key Eligibility Requirements (Details may vary by stream):

♦ Expression of Interest (EOI): Submission through SkillSelect, a government online platform.
♦ State/Territory Nomination: Endorsement by a relevant government agency or Austrade.
♦ Points Test: Meeting a minimum score to demonstrate your skills and qualifications.
♦ Financial Requirements: Fulfilling thresholds for business turnover, investment capital, and net assets.

Invest in Your Australian Future

The Subclass 188 visa opens doors to exciting possibilities. Australia's stable environment, established business infrastructure, and commitment to innovation offer fertile ground for entrepreneurial pursuits and strategic investments.

For the latest information and detailed eligibility requirements, we recommend consulting the Department of Home Affairs website.
