Contributory Aged Parent (Temporary) visa (subclass 884)

This is a temporary visa that allows the aged parent of an Australian citizen (settled)*, a permanent resident of Australia or a New Zealand (eligible) citizen to stay temporary in Australia.

Overview

This visa is a replica of the Contributory Parent (Temporary) (Subclass 173) visa. It allows the aged parent to live for up to 2 years in Australia and become eligible to apply for a Contributory Aged Parent(Permanent) (Subclass 864) visa.

Key Features

1. Live for up to 2 years in Australia.
2. Become eligible to apply for a Contributory Aged Parent (Permanent) (Subclass 864) visa.
3. Travel to and from Australia for 2 years.
4. Apply for a Contributory Aged Parent (Temporary) (Subclass 864) visa in a 2-step process.

Eligibility Criteria

♦ Sponsored by an eligible person, usually a child of the applicant.
♦ Meet the health and character requirements.
♦ Not have a Sponsored Parent (Temporary) (Subclass 870) visa.
♦ Be old enough to receive the Australian aged pension.
♦ Be onshore while applying and deciding for this visa, but not in immigration clearance.
♦ Fulfil the Balance-of-family test.

Obligations

Abide by all Australian laws. Check your visa expiry and its conditions. This being a temporary visa lets you stay in Australia for 2 years from the grant of visa.

♦ Be receiving widow B pension, widow allowance or partner allowance immediately before reaching pension age.

Sponsor:

You need to be sponsored by an eligible sponsor (child above 18 years of age). If your child is under 18, an eligible relative or community organization can also sponsor you.

Role of the Sponsor:

Being a Sponsor, you must help your family members to help stay or settle in Australia. You need to provide them with financial and moral support along with sufficient space to live (for the first two years).

Health and Character Requirement:

You must meet the health and character requirements of the Australian Government.

Balance-of-family test:

You are eligible if you have at least half of your children and step-children who are eligible children of Australia and more eligible children living in Australia.

Assurance of Support:

You need to own an AoS. An Assurance of Support assures the Australian government that you will not have to depend on the help of the Australian government after entering Australia on this visa. This assurance is for other migrating family members too.

Clear Outstanding debts:

Debts to the Australian government if any have to be made clear.

Not be eligible:

In the case of a Previous Cancelled Visa. Visa is not granted if it is not in the friendly interest of an applicant under 18 years of age.

This visa needs to be applied on paper. Also, you must be onshore, but not in immigration clearance, when you apply for this visa.

You need to send more information. Send leftover documents (if any) with your application to the parent visa centre in Perth.

You need to be onshore (except in immigration clearance) at the time of decision on your application. On obtaining the visa (In Australia):

You must abide by all Australian laws. Check your visa expiry and its conditions. This being a permanent visa lets you stay in Australia indefinitely. Being a newly arrived resident, you might have to wait to avail of Australian Government payments and benefits.

Travelling:

You can travel to and from Australia as many times as you want for 5 years from the grant of this visa. In case you want to travel after the initial 5 year’s travel facility. You will have to apply for and be granted a Resident-Return (RRV) to re-enter Australia as a permanent resident. If you become an Australian citizen then you don’t require a visa to enter Australia and can use it to travel and from Australia.

Ensure you have a valid passport or other travel documents to leave Australia. Check your visa validity.

Sponsored Parent (Temporary) visa (subclass 870)

This temporary visa is issued to the parents who intend to migrate to Australia for 3 to 5 years to join their children who are Australian permanent residents, citizens of Australia, or citizens (eligible) of New Zealand. Book an appointment with us for help.

Purpose Subclass 870 Visa Solves:

If you own this visa, you:

♦ Can temporarily re-unite with your children and grandchildren in Australia.
♦ Become eligible to re-apply for additional visas to stay to a maximum of 10 years in Australia.
♦ Cannot do a job in Australia.
♦ Can stay in Australia for up to 3 to 5 years.
♦ Can travel to and from Australia as many times as you wish while the visa is valid.

Requirements for this Visa:

You need to:

♦ Process your application online for this visa.
♦ File visa application within 6 months of the approval of the sponsorship, or within 60 days if you have given permission to Apply in Australia.
♦ Have a parent sponsor (an approved one) before applying for this visa.
♦ Be offshore while applying for this visa, unless you own permission to apply in Australia, that too not in immigration clearance.
♦ Be offshore or onshore Australia while we decide your application, that too is not in immigration clearance.
♦ Fulfil all conditions of the visa and abide by the law of Australia.
♦ Meet any previous visa obligations.
♦ Meet character and health requirements.
♦ Have enough money or access to it so as to support yourself during your stay in Australia.
♦ Pay any debt or have arrangements to pay to the Australian government or to public health debt (if you own).

Stay Duration:

As this is a temporary visa while having this visa you cannot apply for a permanent parent visa. Your stay in Australia will be 3 to 5 years at a time. Your stay can be for the period or date mentioned on the visa grant letter.

Things we need to consider while deciding your period of stay:

♦ Duration, you want to stay.
♦ The cessation date of Sponsorship.

End of Visa Limit:

♦ On the date mentioned on the visa grant letter.
♦ If the sponsor withdraws your sponsorship and in that case if you do not find a new parent sponsor.

Stay Extension:

♦ You can stay for 10 years.
♦ A longer extension is not allowed on this visa. In that case, you must request another visa and apply for the same.
♦ To become eligible for granting of another sponsored parent visa (Temporary), you must stay outside Australia for at least 90 days, unless you have permission to apply in Australia.
