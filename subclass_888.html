Business Innovation and Investment (permanent) visa (subclass 888)

Are you a successful entrepreneur or investor seeking permanent residency in Australia?

The Business Innovation and Investment (Permanent) Visa (Subclass 888) offers a streamlined path for eligible business owners and investors to establish themselves in Australia on a permanent basis.

Key Benefits:

♦ Permanent residency in Australia
♦ Live, work, and invest in Australia with freedom
♦ Potential pathway to Australian citizenship

Eligibility Streams:

The Subclass 888 visa comprises three distinct streams, each catering to specific applicant profiles:

♦ Business Innovation Stream: Ideal for established business owners with a successful track record in Australia.
♦ Investor Stream: Tailored for investors with a significant investment history in a designated Australian state or territory.
♦ Significant Investor Stream: Designed for high-net-worth individuals with substantial investments in complying assets.

Stream-Specific Requirements:

Detailed requirements vary depending on the chosen stream. However, common elements include:

♦ Business Activity: A history of successful business ownership and management in Australia.
♦ Investment: Investment in a qualifying Australian business or designated investment.
♦ Financial Requirements: Meeting minimum thresholds for turnover, assets, and business investment.
♦ Residency: Fulfilling specific residency requirements within Australia.
