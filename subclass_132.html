Business Owners & Founders

Business Talent (permanent) visa (subclass 132)

Business Innovation and Investment Visa

This visa permits you to establish or develop an existing business in Australia.

Basic Eligibility

Applicant needs to be nominated by an Australian state or territory government agency.

You must be invited to apply for the visa.

You need to own the required funding or assets.

Significant Business History stream

This visa is for experienced business owners, to operate a new or existing business in Australia.

This visa permits you to:

♦ Establish a new or develop an existing business in Australia
♦ Dwell in Australia permanently
♦ Do job and study in Australia
♦ Entitled to enroll for Medicare (Australia’s public healthcare scheme)
♦ Sponsor certain relatives to come to Australia
♦ Apply for citizenship (if eligible)
♦ Travel to and from Australia for 5 years

My Eligibility Criteria:

1. Assets and Turnover: Applicant must have assets of AUD1.5 million and business turnover of AUD3 million
2. Nomination: Be nominated by an Australian state or territory government
3. Invitation: Be invited to apply for the visa

Obligations:

♦ It is necessary that you and your family abide by all laws of Australian Government.
♦ Fulfill his/her business obligations.

Stay time:

This being a permanent visa lets you stay in Australia indefinitely from a grant of visa.

As far as citizenship purpose is concerned, permanent residency starts on the day of

♦ Grant of visa, if onshore.
♦ Entry in Australia, if you were offshore.

Family:

♦ Family members can also be included in your application when you apply but before deciding on your application.
♦ Family members coming to Australia must meet health and character requirements.
♦ Family members who are not coming to Australia might also need to fulfill health and character requirements.

Visa Cost:

FEES PRIMARY APPLICANT APPLICANTS OVER 18 YEARS APPLICANTS UNDER 18 YEARS
First Installment AUD7,855 AUD3,930 AUD1,960
Second Installment AUD9,795 AUD4,890

Pay the second installment only when asked. It will be asked only if the visa is going to be granted.

You might need to pay costs for each applicant’s check-up, policy certificates, and biometrics.


Place to apply from:

♦ Applicant can be offshore or onshore while applying and at the time of deciding for this visa, but not in the stage of immigration clearance at the time of applying.

Processing time

Processing times are not available. Applications are assessed on a case-by-case basis.

Processing time depends on:

♦ Number of places available in the migration program.
♦ Individual situations as well.
♦ How quickly you respond to any requests for additional information.
♦ Time to perform required checks on the provided supporting information.
♦ Time to get additional information from external agencies, particularly in relation to health, character, and national security requirement.

Processing time can be long if:

♦ Application is not filled in correctly.
♦ In case application does not have all the required documents, or it requires more information.
♦ Verification of your information takes time.
♦ You need to pay the correct visa application charges to let us process your visa.

Your visa will be digitally linked to your passport so you will not get a label in your passport.
