US-EB-5 Immigrant Investor Program

The EB-5 Immigrant Investor Program allows foreign investors to obtain a green card by investing a minimum amount of capital (usually $1.8 million, or $900,000 in targeted employment areas) in a new commercial enterprise that creates at least 10 full-time jobs for U.S. workers.

United Kingdom - Innovator Visa

The Innovator Visa is for experienced businesspeople seeking to establish a business in the UK. Applicants must have an innovative, viable, and scalable business idea endorsed by an approved endorsing body. They must also have at least £50,000 in investment funds.

Australia - BII (Permanent) Visa (subclass 888)

This visa is for individuals who have held a Business Innovation and Investment (Provisional) visa (subclass 188) and have met certain requirements such as establishing business operations in Australia, meeting financial thresholds, and demonstrating ongoing business involvement.
