HOW TO GET ADMISSION IN AUSTRALIA?

Admission in Australia is a five-step comprehensive process that integrates the following procedure:

♦ Research and Choose Your Course and Institution

1. Decide on the course you want to study and research various institutions offering it.

2. Consider factors like specialization, location, campus facilities, and support services for international students.

3. Consider your financial situation when selecting an institution.

♦ Apply to Your Chosen Institution

1. Submit an inquiry or application through the institution’s website.

2. Check and ensure you meet the academic and English language requirements.

3. Keep track of application deadlines to avoid missing them.

♦ Wait for Application Processing

1. Your application will be processed, and you’ll receive a notification of the outcome.

2. Some applications, particularly for postgraduate courses, may take longer to process.

♦ Receive and Review Offer Letter

1. If your application is successful, you’ll receive an offer letter and acceptance form.

2. Review the terms and conditions outlined in the offer letter carefully.

3. Fulfill any conditions specified before receiving a Confirmation of Enrolment (CoE), which may include paying tuition fees.

♦ Prepare for Departure and Apply for Student Visa

1. Once you’ve accepted the offer, start preparing for your journey to Australia.

2. Apply for a student visa using either your offer letter or CoE.

3. Seek assistance from Visazz Group for any queries related to your visa or admission process.

New CoE

Confirmation of Enrolment (CoE) for Australia

The Confirmation of Enrolment (CoE) is an important document that proves you are allowed to go to an Australian university or college. It is given to you after you accept the institution’s offer letter, which confirms that you want the offered spot.

The CoE is an important part of the process of getting a student visa to study in Australia. Inside the CoE, you can find a lot of information about yourself, like what program you are in, how much it will cost, and how long you will be studying.

How long does it take to get a Confirmation of Enrolment (CoE)?

After you finish the steps below, you will receive the official Confirmation of Enrolment (COE).

♦ Acceptance of Offer: Once your application to study has been accepted, you have officially agreed to take the program offered by the university.
♦ Overseas Student Health Cover (OSHC): Paying the fee for Overseas Student Health Cover (OSHC) for the length of time you want your visa to remain valid for.
♦ Payment for Tuition: Once you’ve paid the tuition costs or the initial deposit that was mentioned in your offer letter.

Once you have the CoE, you can use your Immi account to apply for a student visa. The CoE is a very important document that the Department of Home Affairs needs to process visa applications.

What should you do if your Confirmation of Enrolment (CoE) is canceled?

If the International Student Support office of the university or college cancels your CoE, they will inform you via email. Soon after getting the notice, you have 28 days to do one of these things:

♦ Leave Australia: Within the time limit given, you can choose to leave Australia.
♦ Apply for a different visa: If you meet the requirements for a different type of visa, you can also apply for a different visa.
♦ Apply to have your CoE reinstated: You have 28 days to apply to the university if you think there are good reasons to have your CoE reinstated. You can also arrange a new CoE from another university or college of the same AQF level. Visazz Group can help you with this thing, you can approach for assistance.

The Department of Home Affairs can cancel your student visa at any time after the 28-day period is over.

What are some circumstances in which your CoE can be canceled?

The University may cancel your CoE for several reasons. For example:

♦ Failed to enroll in the course.
♦ Failed to pay your tuition fees.
♦ Been precluded from further study under the University’s Unsatisfactory Academic Progress policy.
♦ Decided to defer your studies to a later date.
♦ Decided to withdraw from my studies at the University.
♦ Decided to transfer to a different education provider.
♦ Completed your studies early.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Question: What does the CoE include?

Answer: The COE has important information about you, like your name, the course or program you are taking, when you start and end your studies, and information about the institution of study.

Question: Can I apply for an Australian student visa without a CoE?

Answer: No, you can’t. You must include evidence of enrollment in study with your student visa application, or your application will not be valid and can’t be processed.

Applying outside Australia: You must provide a Confirmation of Enrolment (CoE) for each course of study with your visa application.

Applying inside Australia: You can provide a CoE or Letter of Offer for all intended courses. But you must provide your CoE before the Department of Home Affairs can grant your visa.

Question: For how much time is a CoE valid?

Answer: The validity of a CoE is determined by the duration of your course or program. It usually lasts the duration of your studies, including any breaks or holidays.

Question: In what circumstances can I apply for a student visa without a CoE?

Answer: You do not need to provide a Confirmation of Enrolment (CoE) if you are:

♦ Enrolled in a full-time course of study or training under a scholarship scheme approved by the Australian Minister for Foreign Affairs and Trade: you must provide a letter of support.
♦ Sponsored by the Australian Department of Defence: you must provide a letter of support.
♦ A secondary exchange student: provide an Acceptance Advice of Secondary Exchange Student (AASES) form.
♦ A postgraduate research student who needs to stay in Australia while your thesis is marked: you must provide a letter from your education provider.

What is Recognized Prior Learning (RPL), Australia?

An assessment process, Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) is used by RTOs to evaluate a person’s skills, knowledge, and experience gained through working and learning. It is through RPL where you can get your work experience and skills assessed so that you can obtain a credible qualification through it. The qualification you gain is based on the skills you learn on the worksite and not the one in the classroom. It makes obtaining your qualification quicker and cheaper. It is cheaper as compared to a full qualification because you’re not paying to learn things you already know.

For instance, if you have worked as a nursing staff for many years, you might already be aware of the skills and knowledge that you would require if you studied the course in Australia. It is through RPL in Australia where you can turn the skills and knowledge you have gained by working in the field.

If you are applying for RPL of the programming units at the Diploma level, Visazz Group offer much-needed assistance as to how to convince them about your true intention to study in Australia.

What are the requirements for RPL?

The primary work requirement for an RPL in Australia incorporates a process where you need to be currently employed in a position that allows you to demonstrate the skills required for the unit and/or qualification. For instance, if you are applying for RPL of the programming units at the Diploma level, you will need to hold a position where you are accountable for planning and implementing the curriculum. This would enable the assessors to see you fulfilling the necessities of the unit to assess your competency. In addition to this, you will also need a supervisor in that workplace willing to sign you off on the competencies required.

How much does RPL in Australia cost?

Recognized Prior Learning in Australia comes at a cost that varies depending on a lot of factors including:

♦ the place of assessment
♦ the qualification you are looking to obtain
♦ the amount of work that goes into each assessment
♦ previous work experience

In case, your qualification is financed by the Government of Australia there will be no additional cost for the RPL process. But, if you are a self-financed student and you have enrolled as a Fee-For-Service student, the cost for RPL in Australia starts from $160 per unit and varies with the institute.

How do I apply for RPL in Australia?

In order to make an initial assessment for RPL, you need to have the following documents:

♦ Job description
♦ A recent resume comprising your work history, skills, and knowledge
♦ Transcripts of qualifications that you have completed
♦ Any professional development or training you have attended

Note that this would be an initial assessment only. The number of units for RPL may increase or decrease throughout the RPL process depending on your prior skills and knowledge.

Professional Year Program

Professional Year Program

Job Preparedness or the professional year is a program framed for career development and intended to enhance a student’s ability. A professional year in Australia means that you would work in a professional environment to develop your skills as a whole. The key intention behind this program is to obtain employment in a student’s chosen discipline by applying technical skills and knowledge in the Australian workplace and culture. Basically, it teaches participants to communicate effectively in an Australian workplace environment. It incorporates an internship to familiarize the participants with all aspects of work practices and it handles the intricacies that would be in the applicant’s nominated skilled occupation or closely related occupation.

If you have a question about how to get a professional year in Australia, browse our website to read more about a professional year.

ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA FOR PROFESSIONAL YEAR

To qualify for the Professional Year program in Australia, you must have studied in the island continent only. In addition, you need to satisfy each of the requirements below:


♦ Study & qualifications: The candidate must have graduated from a course that aptly matches the Professional Year program for which he/she has applied. The applicant must have completed a Higher Education program in Australia, in a course that provides two years credit points over a minimum of 18 months.
♦ Visa: The candidate must hold or should have applied for the subclass 485 Temporary Residence visa. They can be on a Bridging visa or Student visa but they need to provide evidence for the same. The 485 visa holders must have a valid visa for the duration of the Professional Year program.
♦ Skill Assessment: The candidate must have a successful skill assessment (for instance – ACS, EEA, CPA, IPA, and ICAA).
♦ Language proficiency: The candidate must have a minimum overall IELTS test score of 6.0 and not less than 6 in each band to be eligible for a professional year.

FEES OF THE PY PROGRAM IN AUSTRALIA

Request to Defer Your Studies

Are you the one who received an offer of completing your studies from an Australian Institute, yet right now, you are not ready to dive into study? Then, this Visazz Group guide is for you on how to defer your course.

Depending on your course, you may have the option to defer your offer for up to one year. This means that the university will hold a place for you to enroll in your course the following semester or year. Deferment is open to most of the students including coursework or research, domestic or international students. International students on a student visa can only defer subjects in accordance with the conditions outlined in the National Code.

WHY DO STUDENTS CONSIDER DEFERRING OF STUDIES

There might be various reasons as to why students would request to defer studies in Australia. The following are some common ones:


♦ Taking time off to travel, work or volunteer abroad can be a reason to defer your studies. This will allow you to think about where you want to go in life.
♦ Taking a gap year to explore other cultures can make a big difference in your personal development and future employment prospects.
♦ To boost your grades you might want to consider taking a break. A study by the Sydney School of Education and Social Work in 2013 showed that students who took a gap for a year achieved higher marks at university than students who entered straight after school.
♦ To earn some cash, students consider a request to defer studies so that they can build up a savings account is a great way to ensure financial stress won’t impact your studies once you get here.

COST

Some education providers may ask for a deferral fee which might be ranging from AUD 100-AUD 200, which might be refundable or non-refundable.

PROCESS

You can request a deferral through Visazz Group during the process of accepting your offer.

You will receive the outcome of your request through email. We will notify you in the following situations:

- if your deferral application is accepted and approved, a new offer will be issued - if your application to defer is rejected. In this case, you can still accept your offer by following the instructions provided with your outcome response - if you wish to delay the start of your course for longer than a year, you will need to reapply to the university to which you got admission previously.

If you’re an international student who has already accepted the offer and also you have paid your tuition fees, you can still apply to defer if you have not applied for your student visa. If your application to defer is approved, the education provider might do not hold your place in the institution – but they would give you an offer for the following semester or year. A new electronic Confirmation of Enrolment (COE) will be issued when you accept the second offer.
